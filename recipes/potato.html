Potato

Potato is an instant messenger focused on security. It is faster, more secure, more open and
completely free. Available on IOS, Android, Windows , MacOS and Linux

Ingredients

  • Large Potatoes (peeled and sliced)
  • Small Onion (finely chopped)
  • Cheddar Cheese (shredded)
  • Salt And Pepper

Steps

  1. Layer the potatoes, onion, cheese, salt and pepper into a microwave safe casserole dish.
  2. Remove the dish from the microwave and stir before cooking for another 10 minutes or until done.
