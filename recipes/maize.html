Maize

Maize, also known as corn, is a cereal crop that originated in Central America, but is now grown
around the world. When dried and ground, maize meal may be coarse (such as grits) or finely ground
(such as masa harina).

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Water
  • Maize
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Bring the 2 cups of water to boil in a cast iron pot.
  2. Add the salt to the water.
  3. Throw the 3 cups of maize meal in the water, aiming for the middle.
  4. Replace the lid, and let steam for another 20 minutes on the gentle heat of coals.
